Case Study: Conference Paper Management System

The system manages papers submitted to an academic conference with:

  • Papers with unique IDs, titles, abstracts, and PDF content
  • Authors with names, affiliations, and emails (ordered by contribution)
  • Program Committee (PC) members who review papers
  • Each paper reviewed by exactly 3 PC members
  • Each PC member reviews ≤5 papers
  • Review reports with comments, recommendations (accept/reject), and submission dates
